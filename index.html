Ryomen Sukuna!

Ryomen Sukuna, the King of Curses, is a powerful and malevolent entity in Jujutsu KaisenOnce a human sorcerer, he was so fearsome that even in death, his power persisted, split across twenty indestructible fingers. Revived within Yuji Itadori, Sukuna remains cunning, sadistic, and nearly unstoppable, waiting for the perfect moment to reclaim his full strength.
